What Is a Tracking Number & Where Can I Find It?

A tracking number is a unique identifier assigned to your shipment that allows you to monitor its progress from pickup to delivery. You can find your tracking number in several places:

  • On your shipping receipt or confirmation email
  • On the shipping label attached to your package
  • In your order confirmation from our website
  • In the shipping notification email sent to your registered email address
  • On your account dashboard if you have a YangLogistics account

Tracking numbers typically start with prefixes like "TRK", "YL", or "YANG" followed by a series of numbers and letters.

When will my tracking information appear?

Tracking information typically appears within 24-48 hours after your package is picked up or dropped off at our facility. However, the exact timing depends on several factors:

  • Same Day: If you drop off your package at a YangLogistics facility before the daily cutoff time
  • Next Business Day: If you schedule a pickup or drop off after hours
  • 24-48 Hours: For packages picked up from remote locations or during weekends
  • International Shipments: May take 2-3 business days for initial tracking to appear

If your tracking information hasn't appeared after 48 hours, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Why is my tracking number/ID not working?

If your tracking number is not working, here are the most common reasons and solutions:

  • Incorrect Number: Double-check that you've entered the tracking number exactly as it appears on your receipt or email
  • Package Not Yet Scanned: The package may not have been scanned into our system yet. Wait 24-48 hours and try again
  • Expired Tracking: Tracking information is typically available for 90 days after delivery
  • Wrong Carrier: Ensure you're using the correct tracking system for your shipping carrier
  • Special Characters: Remove any spaces, dashes, or special characters when entering the tracking number

If you continue to have issues, please contact our customer service with your tracking number and we'll help you locate your package.

If I do not have my tracking number, is it still possible to track my shipment?

Yes, there are several ways to track your shipment even without a tracking number:

  • Email Address: If you provided an email address when placing your order, you can track using your email
  • Phone Number: Use the phone number associated with your shipment
  • Account Login: If you have a YangLogistics account, all your shipments will be listed in your dashboard
  • Order Number: Use your order number or invoice number as an alternative tracking method
  • Customer Service: Contact our customer service team with your name, address, and approximate shipping date

For the most accurate tracking information, we recommend keeping your tracking number handy. You can also sign up for email notifications to receive updates automatically.
